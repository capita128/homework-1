Cal State LA AAAE Student Chapter

About Us

The American Association of Airport Executives Student Chapter at California State University Los Angeles is a student organization for those who are interested in a future within the aviation industry. Membership of AAAE@CSULA is open to all current CSULA students, regardless of college major and without any discrimination.

  • Our Goals
  • Current Board Members
  • To aid organizational members in furthering their careers and to prepare students to make effective contributions to the aviation industry through internships, seminars, airport tours, guest speakers, and other activities.
  • To establish and develop interchange of information and experience in the development, maintenance and operations of airports with the national organization and with other Student Chapters.
  • To encourage professionalism in the administration of airports through the acquisition of knowledge.
  • To establish both social and business relationships with personnel in the field of airport management and aviation professions.
  • To represent airports and the airport management profession and to cooperate with other on-campus organizations for the general benefit of aviation.
